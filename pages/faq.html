Frequently Asked Questions

What is an eSIM? +

An eSIM (embedded SIM) is a digital SIM that allows you to activate a cellular plan from your carrier without having to use a physical nano-SIM. It's built into your device and can be programmed to connect to different networks.

Is my device compatible with eSIM? +

Most modern smartphones support eSIM, including iPhone XS and newer, Samsung Galaxy S20 and newer, Google Pixel 3 and newer, and many other devices. Check your device settings under Cellular/Mobile Data to see if eSIM is available.

How do I install the eSIM? +

After purchase, you'll receive a QR code via email. Go to your phone's Settings > Cellular/Mobile Data > Add eSIM, then scan the QR code. The eSIM will be installed automatically within minutes.

When does my eSIM plan start? +

Your plan begins when you first connect to a supported network in your destination country, not when you install the eSIM. This means you can install it before your trip and it will only activate when you arrive.

Can I use my regular SIM at the same time? +

Yes! Most eSIM-compatible devices support Dual SIM functionality. You can keep your regular physical SIM active for calls and texts while using the eSIM for data.

What happens if I run out of data? +

You can purchase a top-up through our website or app. The additional data will be added to your existing eSIM instantly. Alternatively, you can purchase a new plan.

Do you offer refunds? +

We offer refunds for unused eSIMs within 30 days of purchase. Once the eSIM has been activated or data has been used, refunds are not available. Please review our Terms & Conditions for full details.

How can I contact support? +

Our support team is available 24/7 via email at support@esimworld.com. You can also use the contact form on our website or reach us through live chat during business hours.
